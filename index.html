Project verification process complete

Feb 18, 2026

Thank you to all PIs who have verified their existing projects and users on the HPCC.

PIs with existing projects that have not verified those projects have had their account and all of their sponsored users’ accounts deactivated.

In the coming weeks, we will be asking users and PIs without projects to ensure that they create or are added to a project to align with ICER’s User and Data Policies.
